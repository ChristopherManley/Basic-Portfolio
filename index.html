About Me

Chris Manley, (aka "Ellen Degenerate”, formerly performing under the name "Team Jaguar") began DJing in Tallahassee's house party scene and went on to run, manage, and headline Tallahassee's longest running and largest independent dance party for 7 years - Menace Beach.

While performing as Team Jaguar and independently, Chris has directly opened for some of the largest acts in electronic music, including; Adventure Club, AK1200, Alvin Risk, Astronomar, Big Makk, Borgore, Cedric Gervais, Chocolate Puma, Claude VonStroke, Designer Drugs, Dillon Francis, Diplo, Dirtyphonics, ETC!ETC!, Feed Me, Gent & Jawns, Hot Pink Delorean, The Hood Internet, HODOR, ill.Gates, J. Rabbit, Keys N Crates, Knife Party, Marty Party, Mark Starr, Meaux Green, Riff Raff, Skrillex, Steve Aoki, Terravita, Treasure Fingers, Z-Trip, among many, many others.

In addition to playing alongside contemporary legends, Chris and his former partner’s music and imagery has been featured in several independent films, on Comedy Central’s “Key and Peele”, and has reached the top of Beatport’s Electro and House music charts.

Chris is currently a student at Georgia Tech, enrolled in a full stack web developer course
